Travelling sales man problem

The 2-opt solution to the travelling sales problem is one way to improve on the graph edges.

An even more ambitious approach is the 3-opt solution and falls under the umbrella term of K-opt improvements.

The higher the value of K the greater chance that an improvement an be found however the trade off is that there are a larger number of candiate solutions to try so it can take a lot longer to find and locate a solution.
