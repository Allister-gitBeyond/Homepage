Cuba

Cuba, an island nation in the Caribbean, is known for its vibrant culture, historic Havana, classic cars, cigars, and significant political history under Fidel Castro.

The journey began in the north of the island in the area of Vinales, featuring vistas that return a sense of stepping into a prehistoric age. Well known for beautiful sandy beaches and amazing cultural influences. Cuba is one of the top destinationss for 2024 with tourists flooding to get a taste of the Carribean!

Japan

Japan's bustling capital Tokyo, blends modern skyscrapers with historic temples. Known for its cutting-edge technology, cuisine, fashion, and vibrant pop culture, it's a global metropolis.
